## Outcomes

- frequency of exacerbations requiring treatment with antibiotics
- time to first exacerbation
- change in quality of life according to the St George's Respiratory Questionnaire (SGRQ)
- change in FEV1

## Results Summary



- Macrolide antibiotics reduced the frequency of exacerbations.
- Improved time to first exacerbation and quality of life measured by SGRQ.
- No significant improvement in FEV1.
