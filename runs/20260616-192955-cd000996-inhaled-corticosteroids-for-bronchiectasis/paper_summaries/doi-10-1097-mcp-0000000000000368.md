# New therapies for the prevention and treatment of exacerbations of bronchiectasis.

## Detailed Summary

This article discusses the impact of bronchiectasis exacerbations and highlights the role of anti-inflammatory therapies like inhaled corticosteroids. The authors note that while these therapies are not currently widely used for primary bronchiectasis, further studies are needed to explore their potential benefits. The paper also covers other preventive measures such as airway clearance and antibiotic treatments.

## Relevance To Review

The paper is relevant as it discusses inhaled corticosteroids for bronchiectasis, which aligns with the review objective.

## Key Takeaways

- Inhaled corticosteroids have limited use outside comorbid conditions like chronic obstructive pulmonary disease and asthma.
- Further studies are needed to explore the role of anti-inflammatory therapies in primary bronchiectasis.
