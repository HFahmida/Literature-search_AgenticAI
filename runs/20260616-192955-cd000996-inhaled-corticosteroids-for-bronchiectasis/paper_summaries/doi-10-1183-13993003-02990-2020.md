## Detailed Summary

The European Respiratory Society (ERS) developed guidelines for the management of bronchiectasis in children and adolescents. The guidelines cover various aspects of care, including the use of inhaled corticosteroids among other treatments. These recommendations are based on a systematic review using GRADE methodology.
